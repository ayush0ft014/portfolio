About Me

I'm Ayush Raj, an Electronics & Communication Engineering student with a strong inclination towards the world of technology, design, and development.

I enjoy working at the intersection of software and hardware, but currently, I am more focused on exploring the coding and development ecosystem. I have hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and I’m also building my foundation in Java and Spring Boot for backend development.

Along with development, I have a creative side — I work on graphic design using Canva and enjoy video editing as a hobby. I also have basic exposure to MATLAB, and I continuously learn and explore new concepts in every semester.

My short-term goal is to become a skilled developer, and in the long run, I aim to build my own design-focused company where creativity and technology come together.

I consider myself a creative thinker with a balanced mindset — not too aggressive, not too calm — but focused, adaptable, and always improving.

While I am still growing technically, I have consistently performed well in extra-curricular activities, which reflects my confidence and ability to stand out.

I don’t follow trends — I design my own direction.
